Ramen

Description

A juicy and delicious bowl of instant ramen that you can make for the low-low price of 1 USD.

List of Ingredients

  • Instant Pack of Ramen
  • Water

Directions

  1. Open the packet of instant ramen
  2. Place your ramen noodles into a bowl and add water
  3. Microwave your bowl of ramen for approximately 3 minutes
  4. Depending on preference, drain or keep the water and add the packet of seasoning
  5. Enjoy (:
