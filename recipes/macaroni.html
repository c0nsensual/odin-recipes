Macaroni

Description

A juicy bowl of macaroni that can feed a family of 1. This easy-to-make, savory bowl of macaroni and cheese will only take a few moments to make.

List of Ingredients

  • Pasta of your choosing, shells preferably
  • Velveta Cheese Packet
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 6 Cups of Water

Directions

  1. Add a generous pinch of salt 6 cups of water and bring water to a boil
  2. Add pasta and boil until finished
  3. Drain the water from the pot with a strainer
  4. Add the velveta cheese packet into the pasta
  5. Stir until the cheese is mixed in well
  6. Add a pinch of salt and pepper to your liking and enjoy!
