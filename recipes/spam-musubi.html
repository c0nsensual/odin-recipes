Spam Musubi

Description

Here's a fulfilling recipe for some delicious spam musubi! It's an easy make and an even more tasty snack.

List of Ingredients

  • 2 Cups of Rice
  • 1 Can of Spam
  • Seaweed (perforated)
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sugar

Directions

  1. Take and rinse your two cups of rice thoroughly in water until the water is clear
  2. Add the rice to your rice cooker, adding enough water to make the rice moist (GIGI)
  3. While the rice is cooking, open up the can of spam and dice it into small cubes
  4. Mix together the diced spam and some sugar and soy sauce to your liking
  5. Fry the spam to your liking
  6. Mix together the fried spam with rice and enjoy lol this isn't musubi
